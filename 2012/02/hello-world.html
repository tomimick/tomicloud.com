Hello World!

Hello World!

Feb 6, 2012

I've finally set up my own site and blog. This has been on my todo-list for a LONG time. Now was the time to do it! Hello and welcome.

Why a blog? I want a permanent location for writing and sharing cool new stuff that I encounter in my software engineering job and in my hobby projects. The level of innovation in web and mobile technologies is just amazing, there is so much to learn every day. I read a lot of valuable blogs myself; hopefully I can provide some value to you as well. And yes, microblogging won't do; I hit the maximum length of my tweets too often.

Another purpose of the site is to serve as a flexible playground for my technical experiments and demos. I will show you more...

Stay tuned for my first real blog post soon.

— Tomi Mickelsson
