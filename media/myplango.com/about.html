About Plango

People use their mobile phone to manage their lives — there's an app for everything. But, how do you arrange to meet three friends at a bar after work? How do you choose which bar you meet at? What about juggling that, plus a trip to the florist for your mother's birthday, and a last minute change to your weekly tee time with a colleague? Is there –really– an app for that? There is now...

Plango makes it fun and easy to make plans with your friends, on the go. Plango combines elements of social media (fun, mobile, social, collaborative) with calendaring (planning and scheduling) in one social communications tool. Plango is optimized for mobile use and integrates with Facebook to make planning social and fun, featuring real-time SMS/push messaging, a native iOS app, and web application. Plango is being developed by GoAct, Inc, based in San Francisco and Helsinki, Finland.


For more information, contact info@myplango.com.
